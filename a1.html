About JPIET

JP Education trust was established in the year 1993 under the dynamic leadership of Shri Jai Prakash Agarwal and Dr. Hariom Agarwal. Trust is imparting qua lity education in professional and other general Academic field - 125 acre Campus, 8 Institutions, over 10,000 students, 7500 ALUMNI. Late (Smt.) Krishna Agarwal, w/o Shri Jai Prakash Agarwal, Chairman of the trust, was a prominent philanthropist of Western UP. Her love of humanity and human cause drove her, taking up social causes and helping the down-trodden in the society. She was of firm conviction that the only way to improve the economic well-being of masses is through education. She started her journey in education field by laying the foundation of JP Academy having classes from Nursery to 12th standard.
